FAQ

Can I bring my own food to the festival?

Outside food and drinks are not welcome, but fear not! We've got a fantastic array of food vendors and beverage stations to keep you fueled and hydrated.

Is the festival family-friendly?

Vibrant Vortex is tailored for a more mature audience. The festival is 18+.

What should I bring with me to the festival?

Pack your good vibes, comfortable dancing shoes, and a positive attitude! Don't forget essentials like sunscreen, a hat, and buy a reusable water bottle to stay cool and eco-friendly.

Is camping available at the festival grounds?

Absolutely! Immerse yourself fully in the festival experience with our camping options. Check out our website for details on camping packages and amenities.

Can I upgrade my ticket or transfer it to someone else?

Ticket upgrades and transfers are subject to availability and specific conditions. Contact our customer support for assistance in such matters.

What should I wear?

Can I bring a bag or backpack to the concert?

Small bags or clear bags are often allowed, but larger backpacks may not be.

How can artists apply to perform?

If you're an artist interested in being part of the festival, submit your application through our artist submission portal on the website. We're always on the lookout for fresh talent!
